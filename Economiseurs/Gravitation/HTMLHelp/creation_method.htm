Creation method

This option lets you choose how the initial star field will be created. Unless you choose a UNIVERSE file, initial planets will be created using initial planet number.

Random, no speed The planets will be randomly placed in space, no initial motion.
Random, orbiting center of mass The planets will be placed randomly in space. Their motion will be calculated so they are orbiting the center of the masses of all planets.
Random, approximately orbiting center of mass The planets will be placed randomly in space. Their motion will be calculated so they approximately are orbiting the center of the masses of all planets.
Random star system A random star system will be created with one star in the middle with several planets orbiting all in the same direction. Some planets may have satellites
Random star system, random direction. A random star system will be created with one star in the middle with several planets orbiting in random direction
Use a UNIVERSE file The program will use a ".UNIVERSE" file to position each individual planet. All the parameters will be replaced by the ones contained in this file. You will find information on how to create UNIVERSE files and some sample files on the Gravitation Web site.
