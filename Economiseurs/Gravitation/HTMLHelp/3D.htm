3 dimensions physical model

This option lets you choose 2D or 3D physical model.

  • 2D physical model: all the planets will be on the same plane, only X and Y coordinates will be used. This is the fastest option. Bitmap will not change in size.

  • 3D model, the planets will move in a real 3D space. The X, Y and Z coordinates will be used. Bitmap will change in size to reflect their distance from the screen.

WARNING: Neither in 2D or 3D model the images reflect the real size of the planets compared to the distance.
