Max collision speed

When two planets collide, what happens depend on their relative mass

  • If one is very smaller than the other, it is absorbed by the biggest planet
  • If the two planets have similar sizes, what happens depend on their relative speed:
    • If their relative speed is high, they explode, creating multiple fragment (smaller planets).
    • If their relative speed is low, they melt to create a new planet.

This option lets you tune the highest collision speed for two planets to melt.
